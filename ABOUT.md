# Mission - Beyond What We're Building

NeoNurture focuses on a core problem in neonatal care: families receive information that is technically accurate but often hard to understand and difficult to follow across transitions, especially from the NICU into outpatient pediatrics. This transition often involves multiple specialists, therapies, and follow-up plans. Responsibilities shift, documentation fragments, and the overall clinical course can become hard to track, even between clinicians themselves, regardless of if the care itself is excellent.

NICU documentation is typically dense and optimized for billing, compliance, and throughput rather than longitudinal understanding. NeoNurture is exploring whether better information design, clinician-supervised language tools, and structured support systems can make this information easier to navigate for both families and clinicians.

The long-term goal is to inform a NICU-specific clinical environment that prioritizes communication and continuity, rather than inheriting general-purpose legacy EMR design.
